Privacy Policy

The privacy practices for Wiki Profile Creations are described in this notice. Only information gathered by this website is covered by this privacy notice. You'll receive the following notifications:

PERSONAL INFORMATION

The kinds of personal data we gather and keep:

In order to serve our users with our goods, services, and customer support, Wiki Profile Creations gathers personal information about them. Websites, mobile applications, emails, and telephone calls are just a few of the platforms we use to deliver our goods, provide our services, and assist customers. The particular platform you use and the particular product, service, or support you use may have an impact on the personal data we gather. Since it does not allow us to identify you as a specific natural person, not all of the information requested, gathered, and processed by us qualifies as "Personal Information." The majority of "User Generated Content" that you submit to us with the goal of sharing with other users falls under this category.

What information that can be used to identify you personally is gathered from you through the website, as well as how and with whom it might be shared.

What options you have for how your data will be used.

  • The security measures put in place to prevent your information from being misused.
  • How to fix any errors in the information that you find.
  • Gathering, utilization, and sharing of information.
  • Service confidentiality.

The data amassed on this website is solely our property. Only data that you willingly provide with us via email or other direct communication from you is accessible to us. We won't give this information to anyone for sale or rental. To address the issue you raised in contacting us, we will use the information you provide. Except as required to carry out your request, we won't disclose your information to anyone outside of our company. Unless you specifically request otherwise, we may get in touch with you by phone or email to inform you of promotions, new goods or services, or updates to this privacy statement.

ACCESS TO AND MANAGEMENT OF INFORMATION BY US

At any moment, you can choose not to receive any more contact from us. By getting in touch with us at any time at the phone number or email provided on our website, you can carry out any of the following actions:

  • View whatever information we may have about you.
  • Change or update any information we have on you.
  • Please have whatever information we have on you deleted.
  • Speak up if you have any concerns about how we are using your data.

SECURITY

We safeguard your data offline. Access to personally identifiable information is only given to employees and contractors that require it to carry out a specific task (such as billing or providing the services that the customer has requested).

PAYMENT

Your name, email address, billing address, and phone number are just a few examples of the personal data we may need to gather in order to execute your order. Our third-party credit card provider alone handles credit card data (PayPal.com or Square).

Your IP address and other non-personal information are automatically logged when you access our website. Only server troubleshooting and website management are done with the use of this information. Cookies are utilized on this website to improve user experience and compile usage data.
